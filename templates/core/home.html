How It Works

Three simple steps to make any lecture accessible

🎙️

⚡

Three Modes, One Mission

Every student deserves equal access to education

🔤

Smart Captions

Live scrolling captions with technical term highlighting, inline definitions, and adjustable font sizes. For deaf and hard-of-hearing students.

🔊

Audio Description

Text-to-speech narration of live captions and slide descriptions with adjustable speed. For blind and low-vision students.

🤟

Sign Language

Live captions paired with visual sign language indicators for key terms. Combines text and visual sign cues.
